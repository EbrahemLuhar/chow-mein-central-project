ABOUT

OUR LOVE FOR FOOD

Chow Mein Central features a warm, inviting atmosphere. To complement our healthy yet sumptuous cuisine, We have an oasis of inviting decor for thr best dining experience.

Late Afternoon time dining is casual to accommodate shoppers and business people. In the evening the dining rooms is set formally and it is time for talk, good food and excellent drinks.

Relax and enjoy our beautiful and comfortable surroundings. Private functions and parties up to 50 people are also welcome.

FAQs

Q.

Do you deliver?

A.

No. Logistically it is very difficult due to our location. We do offer collection orders.

Q.

What if I have an allergy?

A.

If you have an allergy that could harm your health please contact the restaurant directly before you place your order. We strongly advise that you also contact the restaurant directly to confirm your requests.

Q.

What type of food do you serve?

A.

As Manchester’s premier Cantonese restaurant we serve classic Cantonese favourites often with a modern twist to enhance the delicate favours. Our food is always made from the freshest ingredients and cooked to order.

Q.

Is the restaurant child-friendly?

A.

We welcome all families in the restaurant and have fantastic food for everone. Please note we do not have a children’s menu.
